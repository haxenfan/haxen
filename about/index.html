About

Bio

Haxen is a multidisciplinary artist whose work navigates the intersections of code, photography, and print. He is particularly drawn to language, systems, boundaries, and perception, working across digital and analog media. His practice spans from generative coding to artist books and exhibitions rooted in contemporary technological reflections.
