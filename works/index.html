Oversight



They watched, I watched, it watches, the other watched,

and now we are watching.


Our gazes intersect through Google's eagle eye,

behind these gazes, we are both different and the same —

opposing poles of the power machine,

yet equally its interchangeable components.


The deliberately erased faces become ghosts of the digital world,

fuel for constructing the other,

yet also the very essence of reality in the real world.


Reality is torn apart within the operation of the power machine,

reality reveals the fractures within the machine itself.


As the machine turns its gaze back upon its own broken remnants,

we peer through the cracks of reality,

endlessly positioning ourselves within the power dynamics of the image.
